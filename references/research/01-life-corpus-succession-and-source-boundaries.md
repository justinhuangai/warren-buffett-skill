## Source classes

### Shareholder letters
- Observation: Recurring official articulation of business, valuation, and capital allocation principles.
- Why it matters: These letters are the highest-value repeating primary source for the skill.
- Modern transfer: Default to letters before interviews when there is conflict.

### Owner's Manual
- Observation: Condensed owner-related business principles in one place.
- Why it matters: It gives a compact design language for the repo.
- Modern transfer: Use it to stabilize route names and README claims.

### Partnership letters
- Observation: Earlier and often sharper on partnerships, performance framing, and valuation discipline.
- Why it matters: They show the pre-Berkshire architecture rather than later brand aura.
- Modern transfer: Use them for the no-list and competence edges.

### Annual meeting Q&A
- Observation: Useful for temperament, live judgment, and how Buffett handles uncertainty publicly.
- Why it matters: Less edited than letters but also more context-dependent.
- Modern transfer: Treat them as high-value but lower-cleanliness than letters.

### Official Berkshire news releases
- Observation: Best for governance, titles, board decisions, and timing facts.
- Why it matters: Crucial for role status as of 2026.
- Modern transfer: Do not let press articles outrank them.

### Buffett Archive
- Observation: Helpful aggregator for interviews and meeting footage.
- Why it matters: Useful secondary access layer, but not the underlying source of authority.
- Modern transfer: Use for discoverability, not for replacing official documents.

### Britannica biography
- Observation: Useful secondary sanity check on broad life facts.
- Why it matters: Good for de-dramatizing folk biography.
- Modern transfer: Use as a stabilizer, not a driver.

### Major biographies
- Observation: Useful for stitching periods and decisions together.
- Why it matters: Helpful for narrative continuity but vulnerable to emphasis choices.
- Modern transfer: Use to enrich, not to canonize.

### Media profiles
- Observation: Good for tone and public framing, weak for principle extraction when detached from corpus.
- Why it matters: This is where myth inflation often grows.
- Modern transfer: Use sparingly and defensively.

### Clipped quotes on social media
- Observation: Extremely high distortion risk.
- Why it matters: The shorter the quote, the higher the probability that it is framework-poor.
- Modern transfer: Do not use them as principle anchors.

## Recurrent themes that deserve inclusion

### Owner mindset
- Observation: Buffett repeatedly asks readers to think like owners, not renters of ticker symbols.
- Why it matters: This is a structural rather than rhetorical move.
- Modern transfer: The skill should speak about businesses before it speaks about quotes.

### Simple language with hard filters
- Observation: His style is plain, but the exclusion standards are severe.
- Why it matters: That is why the repo must not sound folksy while becoming soft.
- Modern transfer: Translate clarity without flattening standards.

### Compounding through avoided error
- Observation: Buffett often frames success as avoiding large mistakes rather than seeking constant brilliance.
- Why it matters: This is essential to the no-list entry point.
- Modern transfer: Place error avoidance before optimization in route design.

### Management incentives
- Observation: He cares about who runs the business and how they are paid.
- Why it matters: Capital allocation depends on stewardship, not just ratios.
- Modern transfer: Bridge investing and operating judgment here.

### Reputation and trust
- Observation: He regularly treats reputation as economically real rather than ornamental.
- Why it matters: This is one reason the repo should not sound like an optimization engine only.
- Modern transfer: Make reputation a first-order cost in modern transfer.

### Temperament
- Observation: Market results are shaped by behavior as much as by arithmetic.
- Why it matters: A Buffett skill without temperament becomes a spreadsheet toy.
- Modern transfer: Keep patience and emotional filtering as a full route.

### Succession realism
- Observation: The Greg Abel transition shows Berkshire outlives the founder persona.
- Why it matters: That matters because the repo is about a framework, not a cult.
- Modern transfer: Do not write the public repo as if Berkshire stops with Buffett.

### Philanthropy without halo inflation
- Observation: Giving matters, but it should not erase the business architecture.
- Why it matters: Readers often collapse moral admiration into analytical authority.
- Modern transfer: Keep philanthropy in the record without turning it into proof of every judgment.

## Source-boundary warnings

### Do not treat every Buffett quote as canonical
- Observation: Buffett has decades of public speech, and clip culture detaches lines from conditions.
- Why it matters: A public skill must rank source classes explicitly.
- Modern transfer: When in doubt, prefer letters and official Berkshire material.

### Do not freeze Buffett in one era
- Observation: The 1950s partnership logic and the later quality-business logic are connected but not identical.
- Why it matters: Static caricatures erase adaptation.
- Modern transfer: Research should show movement without claiming contradiction where there is none.

### Do not attribute Munger lines to Buffett
- Observation: The duo is often merged into one folk philosopher.
- Why it matters: That blurs where a specific mental model came from.
- Modern transfer: Keep the repo about Buffett while acknowledging Munger as context.

### Do not present title facts out of date
- Observation: CEO succession changed the stable public role description starting 2026.
- Why it matters: Public repo accuracy depends on date awareness.
- Modern transfer: Use snapshot language, not timeless job titles.

### Do not let biography substitute for method
- Observation: Childhood anecdotes are sticky but analytically weak.
- Why it matters: The skill must be run on methods, not legend.
- Modern transfer: Biography belongs in orientation, not as a major route driver.

### Do not confuse performance with proof of transferable judgment in every domain
- Observation: Past returns amplify aura but do not automatically validate every off-domain opinion.
- Why it matters: This matters for modern transfer.
- Modern transfer: Restrict the skill to capital allocation and business judgment domains.

### Do not let philanthropy erase controversy or error
- Observation: Generosity changes the moral frame but not the historical record of mistakes.
- Why it matters: A skill must remain analytically honest.
- Modern transfer: Keep the mistake file as a serious research layer.

### Do not over-translate folksy simplicity into shallow advice
- Observation: Simple wording often hides deep exclusion standards.
- Why it matters: Readers can mistakenly hear comfort where Buffett is actually being severe.
- Modern transfer: Use plain language with hard edges.
