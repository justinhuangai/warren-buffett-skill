# Stop Doing and Circle of Competence

Use this route when the user is attracted to an opportunity but still has not earned the right to judge it.

## Core Move

Start with exclusion, not ambition.

Ask:

- What exactly is the thing being considered?
- What part of it is actually understood?
- What part is borrowed confidence, social proof, or envy?
- What would have to be true for this to belong inside the user's circle of competence?

## What To Separate

Separate:

- curiosity from competence
- popularity from understandability
- price movement from business understanding
- narrative excitement from durable economics
- fear of missing out from positive expected value

## Buffett-Style Questions

- If I owned the whole thing, could I explain how it makes money in plain language?
- Do I know what the major drivers of future cash generation are?
- Do I understand the failure modes, or only the upside story?
- Would I still be interested if nobody else were talking about it?
- If I had to hold it for years with no quote, would I still want it?

## Exclusion Triggers

Treat the opportunity as outside the circle of competence when:

- the explanation relies on jargon rather than economics
- the user cannot identify the core unit economics
- the business outcome depends on a chain of speculative assumptions
- the user is leaning on other people's conviction
- the confidence comes from recent price action rather than understanding

## Response Shape

When answering:

1. state what is not yet understood
2. state what would need to be understood
3. state whether the current best move is study, wait, or decline

## What Not To Say

Do not pretend every unfamiliar area can be solved by "just learning more later."

Sometimes the Buffett-style answer is:

- leave it alone
- keep a watchlist, not a position
- come back only if the business becomes understandable

## Transfer To Non-Investing Problems

For hiring, products, or partnerships, the same route becomes:

- What am I underestimating because I like the headline?
- Am I reaching beyond real understanding just because the payoff seems large?
- What would staying inside my actual competence look like here?
